5 WAYS TO BETTER SLEEP

The next time you are having trouble sleeping, try our expert natural tips for getting past your insomnia.

STICK TO A SLEEP SCHEDULE

Set aside no more than eight hour for sleep. The recommendation amount of sleep for a healthy adult is at least 7 hours. Being consistent reinforces your body’s sleep wake cycle.

PAY ATTENTION TO WHAT YOU EAT AND DRINK

Don’t go to bed hungry or stuffed. In particular, avoid heavy and large meals within a couple of hour of bedtime.

MANAGE WORRIES

Try to resolve your worries or concerned before bedtime. Jot down what’s on your mind and then set it aside for tomorrow.

SQUEEZE AND RELAX

Relaxing all your muscles can prepare your body for sleep.

“Take a deep, slow breath and lay on your back at the same time squeeze your toes tightly as if you are trying to curl them under foot, then release the squeeze”.

EMPTY YOUR BLADDER

Avoid drinking fluids 2 hour before bedtime and use the double voiding technique,

“This means emptying your bladder twice by urinating a second time 10 to 15 min. after the 1st time”.

“LAST BUT NOT THE LEAST”

If you are still having trouble sleeping

Don’t hesitate to speak with your doctor or sleep professional.

“3 MEALS PLUS BEDTIME MAKE 4 SURE BLESSING EACH DAY”

WORLD HAND HYGIENE DAY 5TH MAY 2018

This day is also known as global hand washing day. The day is declared by the World Health Organization (WHO) and attracts patient and their family members to join health workers in their efforts to practice good hand hygiene.

The World Health Organization are raising awareness of SEPSIS. SEPSIS arises when an infection alters the body’s normal response causing injury to tissue and organs.

EFFECTS OF SEPSIS EVERY YEAR DUE TO POOR HAND HYGIENE:

  • 3 Crore People Worldwide.
  • 30 Lakhs new Borns.
  • 12 Lakhs Children.
  • It Kills upto 60 Lakhs People.
  • It Kills upto 5 Lakhs New Born.
  • It Causes 1 in 10 Maternal Deaths.

HOW TO REDUCE SEPSIS????

Hand hygiene is especially important in prevention of developing sepsis. As well as removing visible signs of soiling regular hand washing removes invisible germs and bacteria,reducing the risk of cross contamination. Hand sanitizer should be used in medical establishments as an extra defence against spreading infection.

STOP DIABETES

Diabetes occur when the level of sugar (glucose) in the blood becomes higher than the normal. This happens when the body can’ make the hormone insulin or stops responding to it.

Untreated it can lead to amputations, blindness, kidney illness, stroke, and heart disease. But it can be reversed with lifestyle changes. The standard test for diabetes involves checking for glucose in urine.

If it is found a special blood test- Glycated Haemoglobin –is used to check the level of sugar in your blood.

Watch out for the 4T’s:

TOILET: Going to toilet a lot, or a previously dry child wetting the bed.

THIRST: Being really thirsty; unable to quench your thirst.

TIRED: More than usual.

THINNER: Unexpected weight loss.

OTHER WARNING SIGNS

  • Getting up in the night to use the toilet.
  • Dry mouth.
  • Blurred vision.
  • Recurrent infections.
  • Itching around genitals.

LIFESTYLE CHANGES HELP:

Whether you have pre-diabetes or the condition is established, making alterations to your routine can still lower the chance of developing some of the nastier complications.

WHAT IS HERNIA

If you observe the following:-

  • Feel a bulge or a lump through touch.
  • Experience chest pain and discomfort like acid reflux and difficulty in swallowing.
  • Pain, heaviness and weakness in the affected area –while bending, coughing or lifting weights.
  • Nausea or vomiting… You may have HERNIA.

WHAT IS HERNIA ?

A HERNIA occurs when an organ or fatty tissue squeezes through a weak spot in a surrounding muscle or connective tissue called fascia. It is the abnormal exit of tissue or an organ, such as the bowel, through the wall of the cavity in which it normally resides. Hernias come in a number of different types. Basically it is a bulging of an organ or tissue through an abnormal opening. Most commonly they involve the abdomen. In India, It is very common; there are more than 10 million cases per year.

HOW TO RELIEVE HERNIA PAIN??

  • Use ice packs.
  • Take medication to manage pain.
  • Take medication to treat reflux.
  • Wear a support or truss.
  • Try acupuncture.
  • See a doctor immediately if you are experiencing severe pain.
  • Get surgery.

WORLD HYPERTENSION DAY

Serious Effects of Hypertension are as follow:

Heart attack

Heart Failure.

Sudden Cardiac Arrest - Sudden Cardio Closure.

Kidney Disease

Kidney Failure

Migraine with protein

Paralysis
Vision Result - Blindness

If the diabetes occurs with high blood pressure, the probability of the side effects is doubled. Hypertension is controlled by clinical research, and is being controlled by all the affected families due to treatment.

Normal B.P: Normal blood pressure is less than 130/90 (Ideal B.P. 120/80).

High B.P: That means the Systolic Pressure is between 100 and 140.

Low B.P: That means diastolic pressure varies between 70 to 90.

Preventive measures of Hypertension:-

  • Control Weight.
  • Stress management.
  • Avoid alcoholic beverages and Tobacco.
  • Regular exercise, yoga and meditation.
  • Salt restricted diet, avoid Sweet, Spicy and Oily food.
  • Consume Green Leafy Vegetables, Salad, curd and Buttermilk.
  • Avoid Tea, coffee, cold drinks, ice-cream, dessert, butter, cheese.
  • In the case of Obesity: regular exercise like walking, running, swimming, badminton, cycling is necessary.
