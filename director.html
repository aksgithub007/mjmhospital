Dr. Mukund Joshi

Medical Director

Gen. Surgeon & GI Surgeon

FACS, FICS

A warm welcome from MJM Hospital!

MJM has a legacy of nearly fifty years of service in the gastrointestinal segment. It is quite satisfying to turn back the pages of history to see the thousands of smiling faces. Over these years, delivery of quality healthcare service, which is in line with Quality Policy of the organization, has been the focus area at every level.

We have a highly qualified and dedicated team of Medical, Administrative and Support staff. The Consultants in various departments are among the cream of Specialists in the city. With state-of-the-art technology and qualified and trained staff, we are able to offer world-class services. Employees join our team with different skill sets, which gives us the opportunity to tailor training to meet individual needs. This whole package among other things is what ensures a high and assured quality of service for all people who visit this Hospital.

Plans are underway to constantly increase and improve the services and clinics that the Hospital offers, both in curative and preventive healthcare applications. As always, we are looking to improve our care and service to you. I want to invite you to take a closer look at our services and tell us how we are doing. We shall only be glad to work towards offering you what you look for in the field of Medicine & Surgery.

Wishing you good health at all times.

Mrs. Anagha Joshi

Director-Admin

B.com, MSW, DHM

Welcome to MJM Hospital.

We are a state-of-the-art facility providing a broad array of health care services from routine outpatient facilities to advanced ICU care, surgical services, diagnostic and radiology services, dialysis etc. serving the community around us.

We have a dedicated group of professionals, bound by ethical principles, committed to delivering high quality, evidence-based care through a multidisciplinary team approach. To achieve this, we have created an environment that encourages and promotes continuous improvement in quality and care through professionalism and acquisition of knowledge.

Our core values are a reflection of our beliefs and practices.

Today’s well-informed health care consumer is a discerning customer who wants the highest quality care that is individualized for them. We at MJM take a cost-effective, balanced, approach to patient care by informing and involving our patients in making the right health care decisions. At MJM hospital, care decisions are made in an ethical manner, keeping patient’s interest alone at the centre of all decisions-making.

We, at MJM, strive to look after all our patients like we would do our own kith and kin. It is our proud privilege to care for you and your loved ones.

Mr. Mohan Nair

Chief Executive Officer

B.Sc (Chemistry), MBA,
FFNFE (IIM Lucknow)

The MJM Hospital, Pune, Maharashtra, India was established in 1968 to provide affordable medical care in a patient friendly atmosphere and in a spirit of compassion previously beyond the reach of the common man.

MJM Hospital was founded on the highest principles with an emphasis on compassionate service to people and excellence through education. The blending of these vital ingredients with competence and compassion is an extension of the vision and inspiration of our founder, Dr Manohar Joshi, from whom we all draw our strength and dedication and whose life exemplifies these high principles in every action. MJM Hospital’s commitment to be a centre of excellence both in medical training and the practice of medicine will well prepare our eager and talented young men and women to play a key role at the forefront of their chosen professions.

The nurturing of these values at the MJM Academy complements a fine technical education by enhancing skills with a unique understanding and compassion for those being treated. A commitment to the practice of medicine in its highest form is a lifelong commitment to humanity.

Mr Nair predicts a major transformation in the next 10 years toward more consumer-based health care. “Patients are going to want to connect with their physicians or nurse practitioners on an as-needed basis,” he says. “They want an interaction and they want it now – on their terms.” This requires providers to adapt their approaches to accessibility, scheduling, processes and communication to provide an optimal patient experience.

Mr. Manish Borse

Chief Operating Officer

B.Sc (Chemistry),MBA Marketing

The vision of MJM Hospital is to provide world-class services to every citizen of India, a country with vast disparities in the quality, accessibility and affordability of healthcare services.

MJM developed the concept of a collaborative team, working together to provide comprehensive care to patients, and demonstrating that modern medicine, in totality, could be practiced only through organized, complementary multispecialty teams. The successful application of the concept of teamwork for holistic healthcare delivery proved successful.
