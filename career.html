Walk-in for Staff Nurses B.Sc/GNM

Experience :- 0 - 5 yrs

Location :- Pune

Openings :- 20

Job Description

SALARY WILL BE NOT A CONSTRAINT for the Right Candidate

  • Responsible for complete bedside nursing care of their assigned patients.
  • Carry out procedures of admission, transfer and discharge.
  • Makes beds of serious patients and helps or guides staffs under her to make beds, by supplying linen
  • Maintains personal hygiene and comfort of the patient
  • Attends to the nutritional needs of the patient and feeds the helpless patients
  • Maintains clean and safe environment for the patient
  • Implements and maintains ward policies and routines.
  • Co-ordinates patient care with other team members.
  • Take round with the doctors when called to list new orders and see that they are carried out.
  • Performs various technical tasks related to nursing care.
    • Administration of medication, i.e. tablets, injections. Infusions and transfusion on prescription, or according to standing instructions.
    • Assisting doctors in various medical and surgical diagnostic procedures by preparing patients.
    • Collecting and sending of specimens for laboratory diagnostic procedures
    • Oversee & monitor recording of vital signs, i.e. temperature, pulse, respiration and blood pressure.
    • Performing gastric lavage, administering enema and/or suppository etc.
    • Prepares patients for operations and see that the patient is sent to operation theatre with all necessary papers and medications.
    • Takes care of eyes, ears, back, bowel, bladder, perineum and breast etc.
    • Observes all patients conditions and take suitable action accordingly and / or the doctor
    • Give expert bedside nursing care to all patients
  • . Attends last offices in case of a patient dying during shifts and arrange to preserve dead body in mortuary, or hand over the body with respect to concerned family members, relatives/ authorities.
  • Help the ward in charge to carry out her/his work or act as ward in charge during his/ her absence.
  • Maintains general cleanliness of the ward and the sanitary annexure
  • Supervises the duties of supportive staffs and guides them and reports accordingly
  • Writes the diet register and supervises the distribution of diet report if any, necessary
  • Maintains scheduled poisonous drug registers
  • Supervises nursing care and other tasks carried out by the students
  • Maintains duty room trays, sterilize instruments and see that procedural trays are in readiness
  • Take over from duty nurse of the previous, new and serious patients, instruments, supplies, drugs etc. and handover the same accordingly
  • Maintains all records pertaining to ward/unit
    • Maintains case papers, investigation reports etc.
    • Maintains vital signs charts, intake-output charts and other special charts if necessary.
    • Takes special care of medico legal case papers and records.
    • Writes day and night orders and maintains ward statistics.
    • Enters all chargeable procedures, references etc. in patients file.
    • Checks files for every entry.
    • Enters charges of every single procedure in the patients file.
    • Stores original reports of all company or insurance patients
  • Any other duties assigned by the nursing HOD from time to time.

Salary: As per company’s policy

Functional Area: Medical , Healthcare , R&D , Pharmaceuticals , Biotechnology

Role Category: Medical Professional

Role: Nurse

Employment Type: Permanent Job, Full Time
